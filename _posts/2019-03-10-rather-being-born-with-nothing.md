At that point of the video I paused the video, stand up walking and smiling, that's how strong it clicked with me.

Difficulties, challenges, even tragedies can lead to great growth. That's why GaryVee sees being born with nothing as an advantage. Psychologists call it post-traumatic growth.

So maybe instead of wishing we were born into this and that, we should be grateful for the quests we have to face.
